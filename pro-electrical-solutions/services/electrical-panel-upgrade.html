How long does an Electrical Panel Upgrade typically take?

The duration of an Electrical Panel Upgrade can vary depending on the complexity of the job, but most upgrades can be completed within 1-3 days.

Will I need to upgrade my entire electrical system?

Not always. Our licensed electricians will assess your current system and recommend the most cost-effective and efficient solution for your specific needs.

Are Electrical Panel Upgrades covered by insurance?

In some cases, yes. If your electrical panel is damaged due to a covered event, such as a storm or fire, your insurance may cover the cost of the upgrade. However, it's best to check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage.
